About

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) is an AI architecture that enhances large language models (LLMs) by integrating external knowledge retrieval with text generation. Instead of relying solely on pre-trained data, RAG retrieves relevant information from external sources—such as databases, documents, or APIs—at query time. This retrieved context is then combined with the model’s internal knowledge to produce more accurate, grounded, and up-to-date responses.
